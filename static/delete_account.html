linkup Account Deletion

This page applies to the linkup mobile application listed on Google Play. It explains how you can request deletion of your account and associated personal data.

Delete Your Account from the App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your linkup account is directly within the app:

  1. Open the linkup app
  2. Go to the Profile section
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Navigate to Account
  5. Select Delete Account and confirm

Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by email.

  • Email: ebinsanthosh06@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request – linkup
  • Please include your registered email address or phone number so we can verify your identity.

Request Account Deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted

Once your request is confirmed, we will permanently delete the following data within 30 days:

  • Profile information (name, bio, preferences, university details)
  • Uploaded photos and media
  • Matches and conversation history
  • App activity and usage data associated with your account

Data Retained After Deletion

Certain information may be retained for a limited period for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes:

  • An anonymized or hashed identifier (such as email or phone number) retained for up to 90 days to prevent abuse and duplicate registrations
  • Transaction records (if applicable) retained as required by law

Additional Information

This page does not collect personal data automatically. Any information you provide via email is used solely to process your account deletion request.
