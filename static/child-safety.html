Child Safety Standards

Last updated: December 2025

This page outlines the standards, practices, and measures implemented by linkup to prevent and respond to child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

1. Age Restriction

linkup is strictly intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not permit minors to use the platform.

  • Users must confirm their age during registration
  • Accounts suspected of belonging to minors are investigated and removed

2. Zero Tolerance Policy

linkup enforces a zero-tolerance policy toward:

  • Child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE)
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Any sexual content involving minors

Any such content is immediately removed, and the associated account is permanently banned.

3. Content Moderation & Detection

We use a combination of automated systems and manual review to detect and prevent harmful or illegal content.

  • User reports are reviewed promptly
  • Accounts engaging in suspicious behavior are restricted or terminated
  • Repeat or severe violations result in permanent bans

4. In-App Reporting

Users can report safety concerns directly within the linkup app.

  • Profiles, messages, and content can be reported
  • Reports related to child safety are prioritized
Reports involving suspected CSAM or exploitation are escalated immediately.

5. Cooperation With Authorities

linkup complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations.

  • Confirmed CSAM is reported to relevant regional or national authorities
  • We cooperate fully with lawful requests from law enforcement
  • Relevant data is preserved when legally required

6. Account Enforcement

Enforcement actions may include:

  • Immediate content removal
  • Account suspension or permanent termination
  • Blocking of devices or identifiers to prevent repeat abuse

Commitment

linkup is committed to maintaining a safe environment and continuously improving our safeguards to protect users and prevent harm.
